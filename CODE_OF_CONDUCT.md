# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful, constructive, and technically rigorous community.

Examples of expected behavior:
- Be precise and evidence-based.
- Disagree on technical merits, not people.
- Share reproducible steps when reporting issues.
- Assume good intent and keep communication professional.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, doxxing, or discriminatory language.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for behavior that violates this policy.
